A Critical Regulatory Shift

On 28 June 2023, India notified the Carbon Credit Trading Scheme. It created assets, liabilities, taxable income, and personal liability for CFOs and directors across 14 industries. Most companies are navigating it without a complete picture. This hub exists to provide that picture - for business owners, policy makers, finance professionals, and every stakeholder who wants to understand India's carbon future.
