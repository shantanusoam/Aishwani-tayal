India's Carbon Credit Trading Scheme Understood Completely.

On 28 June 2023, India notified the Carbon Credit Trading Scheme. It created assets, liabilities, taxable income, and personal liability for CFOs and directors across 14 industries. Most companies are navigating it without a complete picture. This site exists to provide that picture - for business owners, policy makers, finance professionals, and every citizen who wants to understand India's carbon future.
